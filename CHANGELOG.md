- Added the `improve-live-consultant` skill and a universal self-check protocol.

  schemas, redaction, evidence gates, scoped active rules, and regression tests.

  telemetry or automatic submission.

  public issue form, CODEOWNERS, and end-to-end learning-loop tests.

  symlink and traversal rejection, stale-preview invalidation, broader secret
  scanning, verified runtime rule loading, and release-version alignment.
- Added signed recurrence evidence, project-local lineage IDs, accumulation of
  new independent observations, and same-lineage deduplication.
