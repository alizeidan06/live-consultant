# Business audit scorecard

Use this as a coverage checklist, not a numerical maturity score. A number can
hide missing evidence and false precision.

| Surface | Core question | Minimum evidence | Common false diagnosis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Buyer and demand | Does a defined buyer act to solve this now? | exact-buyer behavior, spend/workaround, timing, counterevidence | assuming interest equals demand |
| Product and value | Does delivery create the promised result? | specimen, QA, usage, time-to-value, failure rate | blaming marketing for weak delivery |
| Positioning | Does the right buyer understand why this fits? | alternatives, attributes, value themes, comprehension tests | inventing a new category too early |
| Offer and price | Are value, scope, proof, terms, and risk clear? | exact-price response, terms, claims evidence, margins | adding bonuses to an unproved offer |
| Economics | Can each sale contribute cash after variable cost? | price, refunds, COGS, fees, labor, support, CAC, payback | equating ROAS or revenue with profit |
| Acquisition | Does a channel reach the buyer repeatably? | qualified cohorts, channel cost, incrementality, capacity | declaring a channel winner from clicks |
| Sales | Does the process diagnose fit and advance decisions? | stage conversion, call evidence, loss reasons, cycle time | treating every objection as a close problem |
| Funnel/site | Does message and intent continue end to end? | mobile journey, proof, events, page speed, error logs | cosmetic redesign before message proof |
| Delivery/retention | Can the business fulfill at promised quality? | cycle time, defects, capacity, refunds, churn, support | scaling demand into service failure |
| Governance/risk | Is the action lawful, authorized, secure, and supportable? | current policies, consent, processor terms, permissions | assuming platform availability means permission |
| Operations/team | Is work owned, documented, measured, and resilient? | roles, SOPs, handoffs, controls, founder-time data | automating an undefined process |

For each surface record: status, evidence, source date, confidence, impact,
dependency, next discriminating test, and owner decision.
