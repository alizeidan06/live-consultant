# Business setup checklist

Adapt this to jurisdiction, business model, industry, and stage. It is a review
map, not legal, tax, accounting, or insurance advice.

## Foundation

- Owner outcome, decision rights, equity/governance questions, and exit paths.
- Entity, registrations, tax accounts, banking, bookkeeping, reporting, and
  insurance reviewed by qualified local professionals.
- Contracts, licenses, IP ownership, worker classification, and records.

## Offer and delivery

- Buyer, scope, exclusions, price, taxes, payment schedule, cancellation,
  refund/remedy, service levels, and claims.
- Intake, dependencies, production, QA, handoff, revisions, support, and
  completion evidence.
- Capacity, queue, supplier, single-point-of-failure, and incident plans.

## Customer systems

- Website accessibility, performance, security, truthful copy, terms, privacy,
  consent, cookie/tracking choices, and contact/support path.
- Secure processor-hosted checkout, fraud/chargeback handling, receipts, and
  financial reconciliation.
- CRM stages, required fields, retention, deletion, access, and audit trail.

## Growth and measurement

- Source/medium/campaign conventions, event definitions, metric owner, cohort
  reporting, attribution limits, and data QA.
- Claims register, creative approvals, platform policy, customer-list rights,
  spend limits, stop thresholds, and rollback.
- Experiment owner, one changed variable, threshold, review date, and decision.

## Operating cadence

- Weekly constraint review and exception queue.
- Monthly cash, contribution, retention, capacity, complaints, refunds, and
  source reconciliation.
- Quarterly access, vendor, policy, security, insurance, and continuity review.
