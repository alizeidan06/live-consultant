## Recorded source corrections

- The King's Audit worksheet visually reverses the hourly-value equation in one
  location. The book's `income / working hours` formula at PDF 27 / printed 26
  is the controlling calculation.
- The source's EPC numerical example around PDF 228 / printed 227 is internally
  inconsistent. The skill retains the method and uses attributable revenue per
  click plus contribution per click instead of copying that arithmetic.

## Extraction and visual verification

- The 356-page book and 12-page resources file contain extractable text.
  Content and page locators were checked with `pypdf` and representative pages
  were rendered with Poppler for visual verification.
- Rendered checks included the table of contents, magic-wand ideation, the
  17-step problem-agitation page, and the Halo worksheet. Layout and page
  numbering matched the extracted text.
- The 24-page five-ways guide is image-based. All 24 pages were rendered and
  OCR'd with Apple Vision; representative pages were also visually inspected,
  including the cover, contents, Halo/Amazon-review section, and final CTA.
- OCR can misread punctuation or isolated words. Framework claims were based on
  visually clear headings and complete page context, not a single uncertain
  token.

## Source-status rules

Use the following labels:

- **Source method:** a framework or instruction present in a supplied source.
- **Source-reported outcome:** a result claimed by the source but not
  independently verified.
- **Current fact:** a fact verified from a current authoritative source during
  the user's task.
- **Live Consultant synthesis:** an integration, conflict resolution, output
  schema, or niche adaptation created by this plugin.

Never upgrade a source-reported outcome to a current fact by repetition.

## Currentness map

The book was published in 2019 and cites platform observations and datasets
from earlier years. Live verification is required for:

- Google and Meta interfaces, inventory, targeting, attribution, and creative
  formats;
- email authentication, deliverability, privacy, and anti-spam rules;
- current channel costs and benchmarks;
- company rankings, review counts, revenue claims, and case status;
- tools, URLs, and platform-specific tactics;
- any law, policy, regulated-category, guarantee, testimonial, or jurisdiction
  detail needed for execution.

The following are retained as strategic or psychological hypotheses to test:

- buyer-first research;
- larger-market education;
- value before sale;
- strong offer before traffic scale;
- awareness/message matching;
- one job per funnel step;
- milestone nurture;
- diagnosis before prescription in consultative sales;
- conversational, specific, buyer-centered copy.

## Intentional source-context preservation

The source uses both forceful and consultative modes:

- The sales-message system explicitly uses pain amplification, fear, intrigue,
  scarcity, guarantee, warning, and a direct command.
- The nurture and service-call sections emphasize earned trust, value delivery,
  diagnosis, fit, prescription, and not selling a poor fit.

Neither mode cancels the other. `integration.md` routes them by channel, price,
stake, complexity, awareness, and sales motion.

## Copyright and portability boundary

The plugin does not include the PDFs or long copied passages. It includes an
independent structured synthesis, short factual locators, and original worked
examples. A user who lawfully possesses the sources can consult the recorded
pages for full context. Public users still receive a complete operational skill
pack without receiving the copyrighted books.
