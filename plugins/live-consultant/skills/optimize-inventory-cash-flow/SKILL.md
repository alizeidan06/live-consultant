Inventory is cash wearing a product costume. Diagnose where cash is trapped
before recommending more traffic, more purchasing, more hiring, or a bigger
system.

## Lock the cash objective

Record the cash outcome, deadline, minimum cash floor, decision owner, period,
currency, unit of measure, accounting source, data freshness, and evidence
status. Keep two lanes visible:

- `IMMEDIATE CASH-RELEASE`: the next 13 weeks, survival floor, receivables,
  sell-through, purchasing, terms, and reversible actions.
- `DURABLE SYSTEM`: replenishment rules, segment strategy, positioning,
  supplier design, sales process, data ownership, and automation.

Do not allow the durable lane to silently replace the immediate objective.

## Compute before prescribing

Use [frameworks.md](references/frameworks.md). When the bundled
`../../scripts/inventory_cash_calculator.py` is locally executable, use it for
deterministic arithmetic; otherwise compute the same formulas transparently
and show every input. Compute and label:

- expected net revenue and landed unit cost;
- unit, SKU, channel, and total landed contribution;
- inventory age, sell-through, period turns, annualized turns, and GMROI;
- DIO, DSO, DPO, and cash-conversion cycle;
- payload and cube utilization with the actual binding logistics constraint;
- weekly opening cash, receipts, outflows, financing, and ending cash for 13
  weeks.

Never infer usable container capacity from empty cube alone. Weight, axle,
route, handling, damage, customs, product compatibility, minimum order, and
sell-through can bind first.

## Find the cash trap

Test stock mix, age, demand, price, landed margin, receivables, supplier terms,
purchasing cadence, freight, channel mix, sales execution, returns, and founder
dependence. Compare the leading diagnosis with the strongest alternative. If
records cannot distinguish them, define the smallest manual baseline or
inventory count that can.

## Require behavioral commitment before purchasing

Use this ladder:

```text
verbal interest
→ sample request
→ qualified quote
→ written intent
→ purchase order or enforceable commitment
→ deposit
→ paid order
→ repeat purchase
```

Strength depends on cash transferred, enforceability, buyer authority,
cancellation risk, timing, and repeat behavior—not on enthusiasm. Compare
ordering now, reducing, substituting, pre-selling, taking deposits, or delaying.
Preserve every option and weight it by observed commitment and cash downside.

## Protect channel cash while testing the next segment

Model dealers, contractors, builders, developers, direct buyers, marketplaces,
or other relevant segments separately. Compare price, discounts, commissions,
credit days, order size, frequency, acquisition cost, service load, returns,
default risk, and cash timing. Use `protect → test → prove → migrate`: protect a
cash-producing channel, test the new route with bounded exposure, prove
incremental contribution and repeatability, then migrate deliberately.

For tactile, specification-heavy, or color-sensitive products, design the
conversion path around uncertainty reduction:

```text
message or search
→ useful page or catalog
→ sample, showroom, or specification aid
→ consultation or qualification
→ quote and commitment
→ order and replenishment
```

Direct ecommerce remains available as a variant; buyer risk and observed
behavior determine its weight.

## Build the minimum operating baseline before AI

Define the required fields, source system, capture moment, owner, exception
path, weekly review, success metric, and rollback before automating. A reliable
manual sheet beats an automated unknown process. Automation joins after the
baseline can reproduce counts, landed cost, stock age, commitments, quotes,
orders, cash receipts, and exceptions.

## Return one inventory-to-cash plan

Provide the cash objective, evidence gaps, SKU/segment diagnosis, calculations,
13-week lane, durable lane, purchase rules, sales/channel moves, owners,
thresholds, review cadence, countercase, reversal conditions, and exact next
data collection step. Use
`../../assets/templates/inventory-cash-control-sheet.md` when creating a
durable artifact.
