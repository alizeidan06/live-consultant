# Inventory and cash-flow source map

## Knowledge classes

- `STABLE ARITHMETIC`: landed-cost, turns, DIO, sell-through, GMROI, DSO, DPO,
  cash-conversion-cycle, utilization, and cash-forecast formulas when their
  definitions and inputs are correct.
- `DURABLE DECISION METHOD`: objective lock, immediate/durable lanes,
  commitment ladder, segment economics, protect-test-prove-migrate, tactile
  funnel, sales-system diagnosis, and manual baseline before automation.
- `SCOPED CASE`: anonymized qualitative mechanisms from one private meeting
  lineage; not outcome proof.
- `CONSTRUCTED EXAMPLE`: hypothetical numbers used to show a calculation.
- `CURRENT FACT`: freight quotes, container specifications, route and axle
  limits, duties, tariffs, tax, financing, supplier terms, accounting treatment,
  product rules, and market demand that require current primary verification.

## Provenance and portability

The durable mechanisms were synthesized from business reasoning and a local
review of private user-supplied meeting artifacts. The raw artifacts are not
packaged. The shared case deliberately removes identities, locations, private
paths, exact confidential figures, raw labels, unrelated discussion, and
verbatim passages.

Use accounting records, inventory counts, supplier documents, freight and
customs records, CRM/order records, and bank/cash evidence for a real decision.
Where tax, customs, financing, legal, or accounting treatment matters, verify
the current jurisdiction and obtain qualified review.
