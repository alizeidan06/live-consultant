# Inventory and cash-flow skill integration

This skill owns inventory-domain economics and sequencing. It assembles
companions whenever the requested outcome crosses domains.

| Need | Companion | Distinct ownership |
|---|---|---|
| Cross-company root cause | `audit-business` | full-system binding constraint |
| Purchase, liquidation, financing, or channel tradeoff | `reason-business-decision` | scenarios, crux, countercase, reversal |
| Counts, owners, SOPs, controls, or automation | `build-business-operations` | durable operating system |
| Meeting or transcript input | `analyze-business-meeting` | attribution and evidence extraction |
| Pre-buy demand and commitment | `founder-playbook-mom-test`, `validate-business-idea` | behavior-based proof |
| Price and willingness to pay | `founder-playbook-monetizing-innovation` | pricing research and package choice |
| Sell-through campaign | `sell-like-crazy`, `founder-playbook-100m-offers`, `founder-playbook-100m-leads` | offer and demand mechanism |
| Tactile product journey | `design-offer-funnel` | sample/showroom/consultation conversion path |
| Complex B2B sales | `founder-playbook-spin-selling` | discovery, qualification, and stage progress |
| Paid acquisition | `plan-meta-ads` | current campaign, measurement, and spend design |

## Sales-system diagnosis before replacement

Repeated hiring failure triggers a system audit before blaming a person or
declaring AI the replacement. Check role outcome, segment/territory, lead
source, activity, stage conversion, time-to-first-response, follow-up,
enablement, product knowledge, pricing authority, incentives, management
cadence, capacity, and loss reasons. Then compare hire, redesign, enable,
automate, or eliminate options.

## Manual baseline before automation

Automation requires named fields, definitions, sources, capture moments,
owners, exception handling, quality checks, metrics, and rollback. Start with
the smallest manual baseline that can reproduce the decision. Automate after
the process is stable enough to verify.
