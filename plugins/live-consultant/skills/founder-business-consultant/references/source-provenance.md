## Known upstream limitations

- Markdown-only source: no live account access, connectors, calculators, or
  implementation tools.
- No substantive primary citations for many numerical and historical claims.
- Material gaps in corporate structure, tax, finance, HR, operations, security,
  analytics engineering, checkout operations, and formal decision theory.
- The active wrappers preserve the source's bold offer examples and
  direct-response mindset during ideation. Potential execution risks involving
  outreach data, follow-up, financing, pressure, scarcity/anchors, or claims are
  handled only in the later convergence and pre-execution pass; they do not
  censor or rank down the creative set.
- Five broken internal anchors remain in the private canonical upstream
  snapshot. The public package is a reviewed derivative of the same pinned
  commit; wrappers link directly to real files and load complete knowledge
  roots when selected.
