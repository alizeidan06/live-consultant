## Full-strength stakes

Use failure, fear, consequence, and amplified stakes when they make the buyer's
problem vivid and move the decision. Do not suppress the narrative because the
problem frame is emotionally strong. Distinguish observed buyer facts from a
hypothetical or illustrative failure scenario, then choose the strongest story
for the niche and traffic temperature. A hot buyer may still need a shorter
path; that is conversion routing, not a prohibition on the method.

Resolve conflicts through the skill assembly protocol, owner directives, niche,
sales motion, measured outcomes, capability, economics, and current evidence.
Truth status changes what may be asserted as fact; it never blocks access to or
explanation of the complete framework.
