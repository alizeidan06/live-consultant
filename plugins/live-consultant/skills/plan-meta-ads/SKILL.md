# Plan Meta Ads

Before doing any work, read and apply the universal
[skill assembly protocol](../founder-business-consultant/references/skill-assembly-protocol.md).
Identify every skill that contributes a distinct part of the requested outcome,
load each selected skill's complete declared knowledge bundle, resolve the niche,
and synthesize one answer from the full selected stack.

Also read and apply the
[complete knowledge-access invariant](../founder-business-consultant/references/knowledge-access-invariant.md).
Keep every stored method available for full explanation, analysis, comparison,
ideation, and niche tailoring while factual and external-action status remain
separate.

At the start of every invocation, read and apply the mandatory
[Live Consultant communication voice](../founder-business-consultant/references/communication-voice.md).
Use it for every user-facing answer and customer-facing artifact. Simplify the
language, never the reasoning, stakes, specificity, or commercial force.

Before selecting research, examples, or tactics, also read and apply the
[niche intelligence protocol](../founder-business-consultant/references/niche-intelligence-protocol.md).
Infer the niche from available context first. Ask only questions whose answers
could change the research route or recommendation; otherwise proceed with a
labeled reversible assumption.

Treat the supplied Meta playbook as a dated experiment library. Current official
documentation, live account state, business evidence, and cash economics govern.

## Establish access and decision scope

State what is available: connector, export, screenshots, page URLs, event logs,
CRM/checkout data, financials, or nothing. Record date range, currency, timezone,
attribution view, account objective, and missing fields.

An audit or plan does not authorize publishing, budget changes, customer-list
uploads, tracking activation, permission changes, or billing actions.

## Assign launch status

Full method explanations, creative concepts, account hypotheses, and sandbox plans
remain available at every status. Assign `LAUNCH-READY` to a recommended external
campaign only when the user has:

- a defined buyer and proved problem/value;
- a deliverable offer and a claim ledger that distinguishes verified,
  source-reported, hypothetical, illustrative, and unsupported directions;
- measured or bounded contribution, maximum CAC, capacity, and cash downside;
- a functioning mobile destination and message match;
- consent/privacy basis and current policy review;
- tested events, financial reconciliation, spend limit, stop rule, and owner.

Read [meta-current-verification.md](references/meta-current-verification.md).
Current factual claims and recommended external execution use its verification
status; the complete historical and current tactic set remains available for
explanation, comparison, and drafting.

## Diagnose from business outcomes backward

1. Reconcile net new-customer revenue, refunds, COGS, variable delivery,
   payment fees, support, ad spend, and cash timing.
2. Establish CPL, qualified-lead rate, CAC, contribution after ads, payback,
   MER/aMER where defined, and capacity.
3. Use platform metrics to locate—not declare—the problem:
   delivery/CPM → attention/hold → click/CTR/CPC → page conversion → qualified
   conversion → sale → retained contribution.
4. Separate creative, audience/delivery, offer, page, tracking, sales, and
   fulfillment hypotheses. Test the one binding constraint.
5. Attempt to refute the diagnosis using CRM, checkout, cohort, and financial
   records rather than platform attribution alone.

## Build creative experiments

Read [meta-ads-source-synthesis.md](references/meta-ads-source-synthesis.md).
For each asset define one:

- audience and awareness state;
- problem/angle and relevant tension;
- full-strength pattern-break direction;
- complete benefit or claim direction with factual status marked `verified`,
  `source-reported`, `hypothetical`, `illustrative`, or `unsupported`;
- proof element;
- destination/action;
- changed variable and hypothesis;
- leading and business success metrics;
- minimum evidence, review point, stop threshold, and owner-approved downside.

Develop native-looking news, text-message, testimonial, chart, endorsement,
scarcity, and personal-story concepts when they are the strongest attention
mechanism. Keep invented specimens clearly illustrative until the underlying
artifact, person, event, or evidence is real; this status never blocks the
creative route.

## Treat account tactics as tests

Broad/Advantage+ audience, placements, automation, creative volume, launch/scale
campaigns, exclusions, attribution windows, bids, country grouping,
retargeting, and promotion rules remain available as complete methods and
historical variants. A current recommendation or external execution verifies
availability and policy, then compares against the user's control with one
changed variable.

Meta spend allocation nominates an asset for investigation; it does not prove
incrementality or profitability. Retarget with the strongest next mechanism:
missing proof, objection resolution, a different offer, repeated pressure,
deadline, consequence, or another angle matched to the buyer's state. Treat the
choice as a test, not a blanket rule against pressure.

## Hand back a controlled plan

Use [meta-audit-launch-gate.md](references/meta-audit-launch-gate.md). Return the
baseline, diagnosis, evidence gaps, creative matrix, account hypothesis, event
map, economics, exact spend/stop controls, claim/policy review, approvals, and
review cadence. Mark drafts and sandbox plans `DRAFT`; mark publishing, spend,
customer-list, tracking, billing, and permission changes `APPROVAL-BOUND` until
the owner authorizes the exact external action.
