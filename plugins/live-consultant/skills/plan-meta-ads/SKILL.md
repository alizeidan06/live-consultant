# Plan Meta Ads

At the start of every invocation, read and apply the mandatory
[Live Consultant communication voice](../founder-business-consultant/references/communication-voice.md).
Use it for every user-facing answer and customer-facing artifact. Simplify the
language, never the reasoning, stakes, specificity, or commercial force.

Treat the supplied Meta playbook as a dated experiment library. Current official
documentation, live account state, business evidence, and cash economics govern.

## Establish access and decision scope

State what is available: connector, export, screenshots, page URLs, event logs,
CRM/checkout data, financials, or nothing. Record date range, currency, timezone,
attribution view, account objective, and missing fields.

An audit or plan does not authorize publishing, budget changes, customer-list
uploads, tracking activation, permission changes, or billing actions.

## Require pre-ad gates

Do not recommend launch until the user has:

- a defined buyer and proved problem/value;
- a deliverable offer and truthful claim set;
- measured or bounded contribution, maximum CAC, capacity, and cash downside;
- a functioning mobile destination and message match;
- consent/privacy basis and current policy review;
- tested events, financial reconciliation, spend limit, stop rule, and owner.

Read [meta-current-verification.md](references/meta-current-verification.md) and
verify any tactic or interface likely to have changed.

## Diagnose from business outcomes backward

1. Reconcile net new-customer revenue, refunds, COGS, variable delivery,
   payment fees, support, ad spend, and cash timing.
2. Establish CPL, qualified-lead rate, CAC, contribution after ads, payback,
   MER/aMER where defined, and capacity.
3. Use platform metrics to locate—not declare—the problem:
   delivery/CPM → attention/hold → click/CTR/CPC → page conversion → qualified
   conversion → sale → retained contribution.
4. Separate creative, audience/delivery, offer, page, tracking, sales, and
   fulfillment hypotheses. Test the one binding constraint.
5. Attempt to refute the diagnosis using CRM, checkout, cohort, and financial
   records rather than platform attribution alone.

## Build creative experiments

Read [meta-ads-source-synthesis.md](references/meta-ads-source-synthesis.md).
For each asset define one:

- audience and awareness state;
- problem/angle and relevant tension;
- truthful pattern break;
- specific but substantiated benefit;
- proof element;
- destination/action;
- changed variable and hypothesis;
- leading and business success metrics;
- minimum evidence, review point, stop threshold, and owner-approved downside.

Native-looking does not mean deceptive. Never fabricate news, texts,
testimonials, charts, public-figure endorsements, scarcity, or personal facts.

## Treat account tactics as tests

Broad/Advantage+ audience, placements, automation, creative volume, launch/scale
campaigns, exclusions, attribution windows, bids, country grouping,
retargeting, and promotion rules are context-dependent current tactics. Verify
availability and policy, then compare against the user's control with one
changed variable.

Meta spend allocation nominates an asset for investigation; it does not prove
incrementality or profitability. Retarget with missing proof, objection
resolution, or a genuinely different offer—not pressure repetition.

## Hand back a controlled plan

Use [meta-audit-launch-gate.md](references/meta-audit-launch-gate.md). Return the
baseline, diagnosis, evidence gaps, creative matrix, account hypothesis, event
map, economics, exact spend/stop controls, claim/policy review, approvals, and
review cadence. Drafts and sandbox plans may proceed; external changes wait for
the owner.
