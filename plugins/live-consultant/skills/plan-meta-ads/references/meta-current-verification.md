Verify before use:

- objectives, Advantage+ modes, audience controls, placements, campaign budget,
  flexible/dynamic creative, ad limits, and learning behavior;
- bid strategies, cost/ROAS goals, caps, attribution windows, and reporting;
- exclusions, CRM sync, partnership ads, country grouping, and retargeting;
- Pixel/CAPI event rules, deduplication, consent, lawful basis, data retention,
  and regional privacy restrictions;
- special categories, personal attributes, discriminatory targeting, health or
  finance claims, testimonials, AI creative disclosure, misinformation,
  deceptive interfaces, landing pages, and restricted goods;
- account eligibility, billing, verification, restriction, and review paths.

Official product-performance percentages describe Meta's reported populations;
they are not forecasts for the user's account. Treat them as vendor evidence.
