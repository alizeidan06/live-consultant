# Sanitized meeting cases

These cases are original, anonymized syntheses. They preserve business
mechanisms without packaging raw transcripts, identities, locations, private
paths, exact confidential figures, or verbatim dialogue. A case is a thinking
instrument, not measured proof of a universal rule.

## Urgent-cash meeting that drifts into a long-term build

### Situation

An established, founder-dependent inventory business had imported stock,
several buyer types, repeated sales-role frustration, weak inventory
visibility, and an urgent need for cash. The meeting repeatedly moved toward
long-term brand, website, research, and AI ideas even after the owner clarified
that near-term cash was the priority.

### Evidence-aware diagnosis

- The urgent-cash objective and long-term brand objective were both legitimate,
  but they required separate lanes, owners, budgets, and metrics.
- Segment labels alone did not prove the best customer. Dealer concentration,
  contractor opportunity, order size, margins, credit terms, service load, and
  repeat behavior needed separate economics.
- Repeated sales-hiring failure did not by itself prove bad employees or prove
  automation was the replacement. Role design, territory, activity, stage
  conversion, enablement, incentives, management cadence, and lead quality were
  still unknown.
- An “AI operating system” could become valuable, but only after a manual
  source of truth defined products, landed cost, age, quotes, commitments,
  orders, cash receipts, owners, and exceptions.

### Skill assembly

Use `analyze-business-meeting` for attribution, objective drift, contradictions,
and action extraction. Use `optimize-inventory-cash-flow` for landed economics,
inventory velocity, commitment strength, channel cash, logistics, and the
13-week plan. Add `audit-business`, `reason-business-decision`, and
`build-business-operations` for the cross-company diagnosis, tradeoff, owners,
controls, and implementation. Add demand, pricing, sales, offer, funnel, or ads
skills only for the parts they distinctly own.

## Weight and cube discussion

A separate logistics episode concerned dense cargo reaching a supplied weight
constraint while visible container cube remained unused. Empty cube did not
establish available payload. Route-legal weight, axle, handling,
compatibility, customs, damage, and real demand still had to be checked before
treating the space as recoverable capacity.

## High-uncertainty product conversion discussion

For a product where appearance, touch, color, or specification materially
affects purchase confidence, static ecommerce imagery may not settle the
buyer’s uncertainty. Treat samples, showrooms, specification help, guided
quotes, and direct checkout as competing conversion paths to test—not as a
universal conclusion about the source business.

### What this case does not prove

These mechanisms came from one qualitative meeting lineage with uncertain
diarization and no measured post-recommendation outcome. They were separated
and generalized so the public case does not recreate a source-company profile.
They do not prove that marketing, sales, inventory, brand, AI, any buyer
segment, or any channel was the universal binding constraint. Each mechanism
must be retested against the next business's data.

## Meeting close without ownership

A strategy workshop ended with agreement to gather data and improve marketing.
No one defined the exact fields, period, source, owner, due date, deliverable,
or decision the data would support. The correct output was not a longer recap.
It was a minimum data pack and action ledger with every missing assignment
visible.

## Confident speaker, weak identity

A recording had repeated raw labels, overlapping voices, and one confirmed
role. The analysis retained role-level attribution for the other participants,
marked uncertain statements `LOW` or `UNATTRIBUTED`, and avoided turning raw
labels into names. The substantive issues remained usable without invented
identity certainty.
