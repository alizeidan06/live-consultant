# Business meeting source and portability map

## Knowledge classes

- `DURABLE METHOD`: attribution, evidence-status, claim-type, meeting-function,
  objective-lock, decision-state, minimum-data, and action-close schemas.
- `SCOPED CASE`: anonymized mechanisms synthesized from one private business
  meeting lineage.
- `ILLUSTRATIVE EXAMPLE`: original examples created for this skill.
- `CURRENT FACT`: any external market, legal, financial, product, platform, or
  industry fact that requires current verification before reliance.

## Private-source boundary

Two user-supplied Markdown artifacts—a meeting transcript and a speaker-quality
map—were inspected locally during development. They are not packaged. This
skill does not store their names, people, organizations, locations, private
paths, exact confidential figures, hashes, raw speaker labels, unrelated
dialogue, or lengthy verbatim excerpts. Hashing private content would not make
it anonymous.

The packaged cases are original business-mechanism syntheses. Mechanisms were
separated and generalized so no single public case recreates the source
company profile. They record that the evidence is one qualitative lineage with
uncertain attribution and no measured outcome. They must not be cited as proof
that the same diagnosis or recommendation will work elsewhere.

## Verification rule

Use the source artifact only to establish what was said and with what
attribution confidence. Verify financial, operational, market, policy, legal,
and performance claims against their appropriate primary artifacts before
treating them as facts or executing on them.
