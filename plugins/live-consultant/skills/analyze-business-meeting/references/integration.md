# Business meeting skill integration

The meeting skill extracts and grades the record. It does not replace the
domain skills needed to solve what the meeting revealed.

| Meeting content | Companion skill | Distinct ownership |
|---|---|---|
| Cross-business symptoms | `audit-business` | root cause and binding constraint |
| Competing plans or uncertain claims | `reason-business-decision` | logic, scenarios, crux, reversal |
| Owners, SOPs, systems, data, automation | `build-business-operations` | operating design and controls |
| Inventory, working capital, importing | `optimize-inventory-cash-flow` | landed economics and cash conversion |
| Unproven buyer demand | `validate-business-idea`, `founder-playbook-mom-test` | behavioral evidence and commitment |
| Price and willingness to pay | `founder-playbook-monetizing-innovation` | pricing research and packaging |
| Complex B2B sales process | `founder-playbook-spin-selling` | discovery and stage advancement |
| Offer, sell-through, or conversion path | `sell-like-crazy`, `founder-playbook-100m-offers`, `design-offer-funnel` | market-facing mechanism |
| Lead generation or Meta campaign | `founder-playbook-100m-leads`, `plan-meta-ads` | channel and campaign system |
| A caught Live Consultant error | `improve-live-consultant` | evidence-gated correction and promotion |

## Assembly sequence

1. Inspect the artifact locally.
2. Produce the minimized issue synopsis.
3. Use the hosted or bundled router on that synopsis.
4. Load every selected complete bundle.
5. Let each skill answer only the issue it owns.
6. Synthesize one objective-locked decision and action plan.

Do not expand every possible companion automatically. The meeting skill always
requires audit and decision reasoning; other domains join when the content or
desired deliverable gives them a distinct job.
