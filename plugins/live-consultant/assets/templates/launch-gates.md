# Launch gates

- [ ] Exact buyer, current problem/job, and behavioral evidence linked.
- [ ] Specimen and exact-price tradeoff reviewed.
- [ ] Product scope, delivery, QA, capacity, support, and remedy verified.
- [ ] Contribution, maximum CAC, cash timing, payback, refunds, and downside modeled.
- [ ] Claims, testimonials, comparisons, guarantees, scarcity, and terms substantiated.
- [ ] Current jurisdiction, privacy, consent, IP, platform, processor, and regulated-category review complete.
- [ ] Mobile/desktop path, accessibility, performance, checkout/booking, support, and refund paths tested.
- [ ] Events, UTMs, attribution limits, CRM, and finance reconciliation tested.
- [ ] Spend, audience, duration, stop rules, rollback, and decision owner printed.
- [ ] Required legal/professional/human reviews recorded.
- [ ] Owner explicitly approved publishing, payments, external messages, data uploads, and spend as applicable.
